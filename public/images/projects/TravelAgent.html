About me

Welcome to my portfolio! I’m Uday Shankar Gattu, an AI Engineer and Master’s student at Northeastern University specializing in Generative AI, Machine Learning, and Cloud Computing. I thrive on solving complex problems through cutting-edge technologies, with a focus on creating innovative and scalable AI solutions.
